About Me

I am a dedicated Front End Developer based in Alberton, Johannesburg, with over seven years of experience in the digital space. My professional journey has been defined by a deep dive into the Drupal ecosystem, where I specialize in designing styled components, managing complex layouts, and ensuring high standards of accessibility and performance. I hold a BSc in Informatics from UNISA, which provides the technical foundation for my work in creating seamless user experiences.

When I’m not refining SCSS or configuring CMS workflows, I’m likely leaning into my love for metal or spending quality time with my family. Life at home is beautifully busy; we’ve recently welcomed an 8-year-old girl into our family, and I share my space with two cats who keep things interesting. I’m also a fan of board games, PC gaming, and getting creative with various arts and crafts.
